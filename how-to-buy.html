How to Buy $SHADES

Ready to join the coolest bunny on Solana? Follow these simple steps to purchase $SHADES tokens using Photon or Axiom.

1

Install Phantom Wallet

Download and install the Phantom Wallet extension for your browser (Chrome, Brave, Firefox) or get the Phantom app on iOS/Android.

2

Create or Import Your Wallet

Open Phantom and either create a new wallet or import your existing wallet using your recovery phrase. Make sure to securely store your recovery phrase somewhere safe.

3

Add SOL to Your Wallet

You'll need Solana (SOL) to trade and pay gas fees. You can buy SOL directly inside Phantom using MoonPay or transfer SOL from an exchange like Coinbase, Binance, or Kraken.

4

Go to PhotonSOL

Visit PhotonSOL to trade SOL for $SHADES using Phantom Wallet.

5

Swap SOL for $SHADES

In the swap interface:

  • Select SOL as the "From" token.
  • Paste the $SHADES contract address into the "To" token field.
  • Enter how much SOL you want to swap.
  • Approve the transaction inside Phantom Wallet.
Tip: If your transaction fails, try setting your slippage to 5-7%.
6

See Your $SHADES in Your Wallet

After swapping, you should see your $SHADES tokens inside Phantom Wallet. If they don't show up automatically, you can manually add the token using the contract address.

7

Put on Your Shades!

Now that you have your $SHADES, it's time to be cool like the bunny. Put on your shades and hop to the moon! 😎🐰🚀
